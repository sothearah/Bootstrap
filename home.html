Sunset and sunrise, the earth's daily farewell and greeting to the sun, are nature's most dramatic spectacles. As the sun dips below the horizon, the sky transforms into a canvas of vibrant colors – fiery oranges, deep reds, and soft purples, blending seamlessly in a breathtaking farewell to daylight. Conversely, sunrise is a gentle awakening, with the sky gradually brightening from the dark of night, as hues of pink, gold, and pale blue announce the arrival of a new day. These transitions, though opposite, share a common beauty, marking the passage of time and the earth's constant dance with the sun.
