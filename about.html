About us

SunView

Who we are?

Our fascination with sunsets and sunrises likely began as soon as humans were able to perceive the world around them. These daily spectacles are a fundamental part of nature, a constant reminder of the Earth's rotation and our place within the cosmos. Perhaps it was the ancient farmers who first truly appreciated the sunrise, heralding the start of a new day's work, or the nomadic tribes who found comfort in the sunset, signaling a time for rest and gathering.

How we started?

Many of us have core memories associated with sunsets or sunrises from when we were young. Perhaps it was a family vacation at the beach, watching the sun dip into the ocean, or waking up early on a camping trip to see the first light creep over the mountains. These early experiences can create a lasting impression, associating these events with feelings of joy, wonder, and connection. Children are naturally curious about the world around them. The changing colors of the sky, the way light plays on objects, and the sheer scale of the sun's journey across the sky can be incredibly captivating for young minds. This innate fascination can be the seed that grows into a lifelong appreciation for sunsets and sunrises.

The journey of exploration?

It starts with simply paying attention. Instead of rushing through your day, take a moment to notice the light. How does it change throughout the day? Are there subtle shifts in color even before the main event? This heightened awareness is the foundation of the journey. Share your experiences with friends and family. Invite them to join you for a sunset or sunrise viewing. Sharing these moments can create lasting memories and strengthen your bonds with others. Connect with other sunset and sunrise enthusiasts online or in your local community. Sharing your passion with others can provide inspiration and new perspectives.
